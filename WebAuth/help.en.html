Thank you for using Tsukuba Net.

  1. Introduction

    Tsukuba Net an iOS App for auto web auth in the hirasuna and oikoshi student dormitory at University of Tsukuba.

  2. Set Account

    Input your username, the 13 digits number from you student card, and your password.

  3. Login and Logout

    Click Login button to connect the Internet. Click Logout button to disconnect the Internet.

  4. Timer

    Generally, you have to reconnect within 24 hours. Tsukuba Net will alert you before 30 minutes before reconnecting. You can reset the timer.

  5. 3D Touch

    You can use 3D Touch to login and logout in Quick Actions from the Home screen.
